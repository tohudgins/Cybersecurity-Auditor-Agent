ACME CORP — INFORMATION SECURITY POLICY
========================================

1. Purpose

This policy establishes baseline security expectations for ACME Corp employees
and contractors handling company data.

2. Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third parties who access
ACME systems or handle ACME data.

3. Account Management

All employees must use a unique username and password to access company systems.
Passwords must be at least 8 characters long. Employees should not share their
credentials.

4. Acceptable Use

Company systems and devices may only be used for business purposes. Personal use
is permitted on a limited basis.

5. Data Handling

Employees must protect confidential company data and not share it with
unauthorized parties.

6. Reporting

Employees who suspect a security issue should notify their manager.

----

(Note for the demo: this is a deliberately weak policy. It omits MFA, incident
response procedures, audit logging requirements, encryption-at-rest standards,
data classification, vendor risk management, and access reviews. Run the auditor
against this to see those gaps surface.)
